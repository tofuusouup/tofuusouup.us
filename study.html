how i study

Study Tips?

‐ Set a timer/stopwatch
ex: set a timer for 40 mins and try to study for that set amount of time or start your stopwatch and take a break once it hits 40 mins
‐ Ask for help, do not be ashamed cause this is what helps you grow
subject tutors, online resources, YouTube videos, etc...
‐ Plan ahead of time, know what to study for and when to study
keep track of your assignments, highly recommend a paper or digital calendar or planner to help with this
‐ Listen to calming music or sounds (i.e. rain, white noise)
there are all kinds of studying playlists out there
‐ Study somewhere quiet yet comfortable
most universities/colleges offer study rooms and those independent study desks in the library
